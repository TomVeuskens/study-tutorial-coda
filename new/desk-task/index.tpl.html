Similar to the training task, we will take a CAD model and focus on making a parametric specification for a specific part of the model. For this task, we will focus on the tabletop (with its supporting frame) of a desk, as shown below.

You are assigned to do this task in the @@if(condition===1||condition===4){Fusion 360} @@if(condition===2||condition===3){CODA} condition

Task

For this task, we will focus only on the tabletop and its supporting frame of a desk, each of which are extruded sketches as shown below. The sketches for these parts already include some basic constraints.

  1. In the video below, the dividing table between both desks is centered. However, when the width of the tabletop changes, this relation breaks.
  2. In the video below, the depth of the desk is half the size of the dividing table. However, when size of the dividing table changes, this relation breaks.
  3. In the video below, the tabletop has a uniform thickness. However, when changing the size of the dividing table, this relation breaks.
  4. In the video below, the thickness (extrusion) of the tabletop and the thickness (extrusion) of the supporting frame is the same. However, when changing the thickness of the tabletop, this relation breaks.

Your task consists of adding the necessary constraints such that the relations above are always satisfied when the width or the thickness of the tabletop changes and when the size of the dividing table changes.
