Task

For this task, we will focus only on the side frame of the picnic bench, which is an extruded sketch as shown below. The sketch already includes some basic constraints.

  1. In the video below, the thickness of the frame is uniform with an exception of the center beam, which is twice the thickness. However, when changing the thickness of the left seating area, this relation breaks.
  2. In the video below, the 3D thickness (extrusion) of the frame is uniform to the thickness of the frame. However, when changing the thickness of the left seating area, this relation breaks.
  3. In the video below, the ends of the table are aligned with the seating benches. However, when the width of the bench resizes, this relation breaks.
  4. In the video below, the height of the seating benches is the same. However, when the height of the bench resizes, the relation breaks.

Your task consists of adding the necessary constraints such that the relations above are always satisfied when the height, the width, or the thickness of the bench change.
