Introduction to the basics of Autodesk Fusion 360 (optional)

Feature-based solid modeling

Autodesk Fusion 360 is a feature-based 3D solid modeling application. This means that 2D sketches are turned into 3D solid models using modeling features, such as "Extrude", "Revolve", "Sweep", etc. The benefit of feature-based modeling approaches is that the resulting 3D geometry can easily be altered by changing the underlying sketches/features.
