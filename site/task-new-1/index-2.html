Study task 1: CODA

You are assigned to do this task in the CODA condition

Similar to the training task, you are given a CAD model that currently behaves as shown in the left side of the instruction video, but should behave as shown in the right side of the video.

Your tasks consists of adding the necessary constraints such that the model behaves as shown in the right side of the video when changing the TODO parameter.
